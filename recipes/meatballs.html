Meatballs

Description

A bunch of balls made of meat, onion and herbs

Ingredients

  • 300g of minced meat
  • 1 onion
  • One egg yolk
  • 5 spoons of breadcrumbs
  • 3 spoons of sunflower oil
  • 1 spoon of paprika
  • 1 spoon of cumin
  • A bunch of cilantro or parsley

Steps

  1. Chop the onion into very small pieces and add it to the minced meat in a salad bowl
  2. Add the egg yolk, breadcrumbs and 3 tablespoons of sunflower oil, mix
  3. Add the paprika, cumin and coriander then season with salt and pepper, stir well and mix by hand
  4. Form balls (do not make them too big). Put some oil in a frying pan and cook the meatballs for about 10 minutes (do not forget to turn them from time to time)
