Tiramisu

Description

An italian dessert with mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, coffee and cocoa powder

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • cocoa powder
  • 2 spoons of Amaretto or Marsalla or Rhum
  • 20cl of coffee
  • spoon biscuits
  • 100g of sugar

Steps

  1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites. Pour the egg yolks into a bowl with the sugar, stir then add the mascarpone. You can also add a little vanilla sugar here
  2. beat the egg whites with a mixer (if you don't have one you can do it by hand with a whisk but it will be more difficult, it may be useful to add a few drops of lemon and a little sugar to the blanks for your convenience)
  3. Incorporate the egg whites into the previous mixture using a spatula, stirring gently (avoid “breaking” the egg whites)
  4. Heat the coffee, add the Amaretto and soak the biscuits in it
  5. In the bottom of a dish, place a layer of biscuits and cover with the mixture containing the egg whites and yolks, repeat the operation and finally cover with a layer of cocoa
  6. Put in the fridge for about 6 hours
