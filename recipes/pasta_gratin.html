Pasta gratin

Description

An meal of cooked pasta with cheese

Ingredients

  • 400g of Penne rigate
  • A bunch of parsley
  • Oil
  • One or 2 goat cheese
  • 1 egg
  • emmental cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • An half liter of water

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta, salting the water, then drain and leave to cool
  2. Cut and chop the parsley, fry in a little oil. Add the pasta once cooled then the goat cheese cheese and mix
  3. Add the egg, salt, pepper and mix again
  4. Put the preparation in a gratin dish, add half a liter of water, sprinkle with grated Emmental and bake for about 35 minutes at 180 degrees
